Industrial Shed vs PEB Structure: Which One Should You Choose?

Factory owners in Delhi NCR often ask: "Should I go for a regular industrial shed or a PEB structure?" This guide gives you the honest, detailed answer.

You have land. You need a structure for your factory, warehouse, or production unit. Two options come up repeatedly — the classic Industrial Shed (MS structure) and the modern PEB (Pre Engineered Building). Both work, but for different situations.

At R.R Teen Set Solution, we build both. So we can give you an unbiased comparison based on real projects across Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, and Faridabad.

💡 Quick Answer: Industrial Shed is best for smaller projects (up to 20m span, basic use). PEB is better for large factories, wide clear spans (20–90m), and long-term investment.

What is an Industrial Shed?

An industrial shed (also called MS structure shed) is a steel-framed structure built using mild steel (MS) columns, beams, purlins, and GI sheet roofing. It's the traditional method used for decades across Delhi NCR.

It works well for factories, workshops, godowns, and storage units where the span needed is up to 20–25 meters.

What is a PEB Structure?

PEB (Pre Engineered Building) is a modern construction method where the entire steel structure is designed using software, fabricated in a factory, and assembled on-site. Components are precision-made and bolt-connected — no welding on site.

PEB is ideal for large clear spans (20m to 90m+), multi-bay factories, and projects where speed and future expandability matter.

When to Choose an Industrial Shed

Go with an industrial MS shed if:

  • Your area requirement is up to 5,000–8,000 sq.ft
  • You need clear span of 15–20m (no middle columns)
  • Budget is the primary concern
  • Timeline is short — need ready in 3–4 weeks
  • It's a workshop, small factory, or storage godown
  • You're in Delhi NCR and want fast, affordable fabrication

When to Choose a PEB Structure

Go with PEB if:

  • Area requirement is 10,000+ sq.ft
  • You need large clear spans (25–90m) — no middle columns for big machinery
  • You plan to expand the building in future
  • You need crane support (5 ton and above)
  • Long-term durability is the priority
  • You're setting up a large manufacturing or logistics unit

Cost Difference: Is PEB Actually More Expensive?

This is where people get confused. PEB costs slightly more per sq.ft — but the total project cost can actually be 15–25% less than MS shed for large projects. Why? Because:

  • Foundation savings: PEB is lighter — smaller foundation needed
  • Labour savings: Factory-fabricated, faster assembly on site
  • Material efficiency: Computer-designed = zero wasted steel
  • Maintenance savings: PEB needs less maintenance over 30 years

📊 Example: For a 20,000 sq.ft factory in Noida — Industrial Shed may cost ₹56–60 lakh, while PEB may cost ₹48–55 lakh total (including foundation savings). PEB wins on cost for large projects!

Which One Should You Choose?

Here's our simple recommendation:

  • Small to medium project (up to 10,000 sq.ft): Industrial Shed — faster, more affordable
  • Large project (10,000 sq.ft+): PEB Structure — better value, wider spans, expandable
