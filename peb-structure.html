PEB vs RCC

Why Choose PEB over RCC?

❌ Traditional RCC Building

  • Construction time: 6–12 months
  • Higher cost: ₹800–1200/sq.ft
  • Limited clear span: max 15–20m
  • Cannot be relocated or expanded easily
  • Heavy foundation needed

✅ PEB Structure

  • Construction time: 2–4 months
  • Lower cost: ₹220–350/sq.ft
  • Clear span up to 90m
  • Easily relocatable and expandable
  • Light foundation, saves cost

FAQ

PEB Structure Questions

PEB structure ka kharcha kitna aata hai Delhi NCR mein?

Delhi NCR mein PEB structure ₹220 se ₹420/sq.ft mein banta hai. 10,000 sq.ft ki large factory ke liye PEB ₹22–35 lakh mein complete hota hai — jo RCC se 30–40% sasta hai. Free design consultation ke liye contact karein.

PEB structure kitne time mein complete hota hai?

PEB construction RCC se 50% faster hota hai. Small PEB (up to 5000 sqft) 25–35 din mein, medium (5000–20,000 sqft) 35–55 din mein, aur large structures 50–80 din mein complete ho jaate hain.

Kya PEB structure earthquake proof hota hai?

Haan! Hamare PEB structures IS 800 aur IS 1893 (seismic) standards ke according design kiye jaate hain. Steel ki flexibility earthquake energy ko absorb karti hai — RCC se zyada safe hota hai.
