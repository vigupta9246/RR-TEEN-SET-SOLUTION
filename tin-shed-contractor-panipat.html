Tin Shed & Steel Structure Contractor in Panipat

Steel sheds and godown structures built for Panipat's textile, handloom and warehousing units, with GST-compliant billing.

Textile HubHSIIDC Industrial EstateGodown Storage

Panipat's identity as India's textile and handloom hub means most of its industrial shed demand comes from weaving units, dyeing units and large storage godowns — spaces that need good ventilation, dust control and large clear-span floors more than office-style finishing. R.R Teen Set Solution has built sheds specifically configured for these needs, working with mill owners who need durable roofing that can handle constant machine vibration and heat.

💡 Quick Answer: Tin shed cost in Panipat ranges from ₹180 to ₹250/sq.ft depending on size and whether insulation or ventilation upgrades are included.

Why Panipat Chooses R.R Teen Set Solution

Textile and dyeing units in Panipat generate more heat and humidity than a typical factory shed, so we recommend and install turbo ventilators and, where needed, insulated roofing sheets to manage internal temperature. For godown and cold-storage-adjacent structures, we also focus on tighter sheet overlaps to reduce moisture ingress — a common complaint we hear from Panipat clients about previous contractors' work.
