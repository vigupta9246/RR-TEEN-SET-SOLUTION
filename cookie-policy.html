🍪 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — like your preferences or consent choices.

Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or install software. They simply store small pieces of text that websites can read on your next visit.

⚙️ 2. How We Use Cookies

R.R Teen Set Solution uses cookies for three purposes:

  • Necessary: To make the website work (navigation, form submissions, security)
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • Marketing: To show relevant ads and track marketing campaign performance
Your choice matters: We only load Analytics and Marketing cookies after you give explicit consent. You can accept, reject, or customize your preferences at any time.
