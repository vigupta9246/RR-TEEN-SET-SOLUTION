Roofing Materials We Work With

  • Corrugated GI Sheets: Most common, cost-effective, durable with proper maintenance.
  • Colour-coated roofing sheets: Pre-painted GI sheets in various colours for a more finished look.
  • PUF/Sandwich insulated panels: Best heat and sound insulation, used for occupied spaces and cold storage.
  • Polycarbonate sheets: Light-transmitting roofing for sit-outs, walkways, and skylights.

How to Choose a Roofing Contractor in Delhi NCR

  • Ask for GST invoicing: A properly registered contractor provides GST invoices — this protects you and confirms they're a legitimate business.
  • Check material grade: Ask specifically for IS-standard steel and sheets, not generic "local" material.
  • Get a written quote: Verbal quotes lead to disputes later — always get scope and pricing in writing.
  • Ask about drainage/slope planning: Poor slope design is the #1 cause of roofing leakage complaints we see on other contractors' work.
  • Check past project photos: A contractor with 500+ completed projects should be able to show real work, not just stock images.
