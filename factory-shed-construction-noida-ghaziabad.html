Factory Shed Construction in Noida & Ghaziabad: 2026 Complete Guide

Noida and Ghaziabad are among the fastest-growing industrial areas in NCR. If you're setting up a factory here, this guide covers everything — cost, permissions, contractor selection, and timeline.

Noida (especially Noida Expressway, Sectors 57–165, and Greater Noida) and Ghaziabad (Loni, Sahibabad, Modinagar) have seen massive industrial growth in recent years. Thousands of businesses have set up manufacturing units, warehouses, and workshops here — and the factory shed is the most common structure they choose.

If you're planning factory shed construction in Noida or Ghaziabad in 2026, this guide is for you.

Why Factory Sheds Are Popular in Noida & Ghaziabad

  • Industrial zones with proper land use permissions (industrial plots)
  • Lower land cost than central Delhi — bigger area possible
  • Excellent road connectivity (NH-9, NH-24, Yamuna Expressway)
  • Growing market for manufacturing, logistics, and e-commerce
  • Steel sheds can be built in 3–6 weeks — ideal for quick business setup

Permissions for Factory Shed in Noida

If your plot is in an industrial zone in Noida (GNIDA allotted), here's what you typically need:

  • Building plan approval from GNIDA or NIDA depending on area
  • Fire NOC from UP Fire Department (for factories above certain size)
  • Pollution clearance (if your manufacturing involves emissions)
  • Power connection — PVVNL for industrial load

Process tip: If you have a leased GNIDA plot, the lessor often helps with permission documentation. R.R Teen Set Solution can guide you through this process.

Permissions for Factory Shed in Ghaziabad

For Ghaziabad (GDA controlled areas, industrial zones in Sahibabad, Loni, Modinagar):

  • Building permission from GDA or local Nagar Panchayat
  • Fire NOC from Ghaziabad fire department
  • UPPCB clearance for manufacturing units

Important: Always verify land use of your plot before starting construction. Industrial shed on residential land is illegal and can be demolished.

Factory Shed Timeline — Noida & Ghaziabad

  • Site preparation + foundation: 5–10 days
  • Column erection: 2–4 days
  • Beam and purlin fitting: 3–5 days
  • Roofing and cladding: 4–8 days
  • Finishing (doors, windows, paint): 3–5 days
  • Total for 5,000 sq.ft factory: 18–25 working days

5 Contractor Tips Specific to Noida & Ghaziabad

  1. Choose a contractor registered in Delhi NCR with UP project experience
  2. Verify they have done projects in industrial areas (access, material delivery)
  3. Confirm they understand local soil conditions (Yamuna flood plain areas need deeper foundation)
  4. Check if they handle local labor and compliance — saves you coordination time
  5. Get a UP GST compliant invoice — UP businesses may need UP-GSTIN for ITC

Conclusion

Factory shed construction in Noida and Ghaziabad is straightforward if you have the right contractor and understand the permissions process. The industrial zones in both cities offer excellent locations for manufacturing, logistics, and warehousing — and steel sheds are the fastest way to get operational.
