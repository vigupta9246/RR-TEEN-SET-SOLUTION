FAQ

Warehouse Questions

Prefab warehouse banwane mein kitna kharcha aata hai Delhi mein?

Delhi NCR mein prefab warehouse ka rate ₹185 se ₹280/sq.ft hota hai. 5000 sq.ft ka medium warehouse approx ₹9–12 lakh mein tayaar ho jaata hai. Cold storage structure thoda mahanga hota hai — ₹12–16 lakh for 5000 sq.ft. Contact karein free quote ke liye.

Kya warehouse mein forklift chal sakti hai?

Haan! Hum forklift-compatible warehouses banate hain — RCC flooring recommendation ke saath aur adequate clear height (7–12m) ke saath. Loading bays aur dock levelers bhi add kar sakte hain.

Delivery kitne din mein hogi?

Small warehouse (up to 3000 sq.ft) 15–20 din mein, medium (3000–10,000 sq.ft) 20–35 din mein, aur large warehouse 35–55 din mein tayaar ho jaata hai. Written timeline commitment dete hain.
