FAQ

Common Questions

Delhi mein tin shed banane ka kharcha kitna hai?

Delhi NCR mein tin shed ka kharcha approximately ₹150–220 per square feet hota hai. 1000 sqft tin shed ke liye ₹1.5L–₹2.2L, aur 2000 sqft ke liye ₹3L–₹4.4L lagta hai. Exact cost material quality, design, aur location pe depend karta hai.

Industrial shed cost per sqft kya hai?

Delhi NCR mein industrial shed ka cost ₹200–300 per square feet hota hai. Isme steel structure, cladding sheets, aur basic fittings included hain. Complex design ya heavy-duty structure thoda zyada cost karega.

PEB structure aur tin shed mein kya difference hai?

Tin shed traditional steel frame pe banaya jaata hai — ₹150–220/sqft, chhote areas ke liye best. PEB (Pre Engineered Building) factory-manufactured structure hoti hai — ₹280–380/sqft, large spans (50ft+) ke liye best, zyada durable aur faster installation.

Shed banane mein kitna time lagta hai?

Tin shed 7–15 din mein complete hoti hai. Industrial shed 15–30 din mein. PEB structure 3–6 hafte mein ready ho jaata hai size ke according. R.R Teen Set Solution time pe delivery guarantee karta hai.
