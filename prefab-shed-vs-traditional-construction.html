Prefab Shed vs Traditional Construction: Which Is Right for Your Delhi NCR Business?

Traditional brick-and-mortar or modern prefab steel shed — which makes more sense for your Delhi NCR business? We break down cost, time, durability, and long-term value with real numbers.

When business owners in Delhi NCR need a new building — a factory, warehouse, office, or workshop — they often default to traditional RCC (brick, concrete, steel bars) construction. It's what they've always seen. But prefab steel sheds have become dramatically better and more affordable. Should you consider switching?

Let's look at this honestly with actual cost and timeline data from Delhi NCR projects in 2026.

💡 Summary: For single-storey industrial and commercial structures in Delhi NCR, prefab steel sheds beat traditional construction on cost (60–70% cheaper), speed (4–5x faster), and flexibility. Traditional construction is better only for multi-storey residential or permanent institutional buildings.

Cost Comparison (Per Sq.Ft)

📊 Cost per sq.ft — Delhi NCR 2026

Traditional RCC construction₹800–1,400/sq.ft
Prefab steel shed₹185–280/sq.ft

Construction Timeline

⏱️ Time to Complete — 5,000 sq.ft Structure

Traditional RCC construction5–8 months
Prefab steel shed3–5 weeks

Those extra months of traditional construction mean months of delayed operations, continuing rent costs elsewhere, or lost production. At ₹50,000/month rent, a 6-month delay costs ₹3 lakh — before you even start operations.

Durability and Maintenance

The common belief is that RCC lasts longer and requires less maintenance. Let's test this:

  • Traditional RCC: Lasts 40–60 years (structure). But waterproofing fails every 7–10 years (costly). Cracks appear within 5–10 years in Delhi NCR's extreme climate. Repair costs are high.
  • Prefab steel shed: IS-grade steel with anti-rust coating lasts 20–30 years. Annual maintenance cost is low (anti-rust paint, screw checks). No major structural repairs in 20 years if properly maintained.

Flexibility and Future Use

This is where prefab completely wins:

  • Steel shed can be expanded by adding more bays — no demolition
  • Can be relocated if you move your business
  • Can be sold and reinstalled at another location
  • Internal layout can be changed without structural work

Traditional RCC is permanent. Any change requires expensive and messy demolition and reconstruction.

When Traditional Construction Is Better

Traditional RCC or brick construction makes sense when:

  • You're building multi-storey (3+ floors) residential or commercial building
  • You need the structure to last 50+ years without any modification
  • Zoning laws require RCC construction in your area
  • You need very high fire resistance (RCC offers better passive fire protection)

Conclusion

For the vast majority of Delhi NCR businesses — factories, workshops, warehouses, godowns, commercial spaces — a prefab steel shed is the better choice in 2026. It costs less, builds faster, and gives you more flexibility. The only reason to choose traditional construction is for multi-storey structures or when regulations require it.
