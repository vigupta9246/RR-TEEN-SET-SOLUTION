Tin Shed Ka Kharcha Kitna Hota Hai Delhi Mein?

Delhi NCR mein tin shed banwane ka kharcha kai factors pe depend karta hai — size, material quality, aur location. Is guide mein hum detailed breakdown denge taki aap sahi budget plan kar sakein.

Rate Chart 2026:

Basic Tin Shed₹180–220/sq.ft
Industrial Shed₹200–250/sq.ft
PEB Structure₹220–280/sq.ft
Prefab Warehouse₹185–230/sq.ft

Example: 1000 sq.ft industrial shed ka kharcha hoga approx ₹2,00,000 – ₹2,50,000 (material + labor + installation). GST extra.

Tip: Add-ons jaise insulation sheet (+₹15/sqft), turbo ventilator (+₹8/sqft), anti-rust coating (+₹25/sqft) se total thoda badh jaata hai lekin long-term mein fayda hota hai.

Guide · March 2026

Industrial Shed vs PEB Structure — Kya Farak Hai?

Factory ya warehouse ke liye shed choose karna confusing ho sakta hai. Yahan hum Industrial Shed aur PEB (Pre Engineered Building) ka comparison karenge taki aap sahi choice kar sakein.

INDUSTRIAL SHED
✅ Low cost
✅ Simple design
✅ Quick build
⚡ Up to 60m span
PEB STRUCTURE
✅ Large spans
✅ Custom design
✅ Stronger
⚡ Up to 90m span

Conclusion: Agar budget limited hai aur simple warehouse chahiye — Industrial Shed. Agar large manufacturing unit ya long-term investment chahiye — PEB better option hai.

Agricultural Shed ke Liye Best Options Delhi NCR Mein

Poultry farm, dairy unit, ya crop storage ke liye shed chahiye? Agricultural sheds alag requirements hoti hain — ventilation, height, aur weather resistance critical hain.

Key Requirements:

  • Good ventilation — turbo ventilators recommended
  • Height minimum 12-14 ft for poultry
  • Anti-rust coating for humid environments
  • Wide span — no middle columns
  • Affordable rate — ₹175-210/sq.ft
