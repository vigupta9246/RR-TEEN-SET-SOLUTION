FAQ

Industrial Shed Questions

Industrial shed ka kharcha kitna hota hai Delhi mein?

Delhi NCR mein industrial shed ka rate ₹200 se ₹320/sq.ft hota hai — shed size, span, aur height ke hisaab se. 5000 sq.ft industrial shed ka total kharcha approx ₹10–16 lakh hota hai (material + labor + installation). Free site visit pe exact quote milega.

Kya overhead crane support possible hai?

Haan! Hum overhead EOT crane support ke saath industrial sheds banate hain — 2 ton se 10 ton capacity tak. Crane girder, runway beams sab design mein include kar sakte hain.

Maximum kitna span ho sakta hai?

R.R Teen Set Solution 60m clear span tak ke industrial sheds banata hai — bina beech mein column ke. Aur bade spans ke liye PEB (Pre Engineered Building) structure recommend karte hain jo 90m+ tak possible hai.
